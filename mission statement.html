The Mission of the School of Computing is:

Provide quality undergraduate and graduate education in both the theoritical and applied foundations of Computing and train students to effectively apply this education to solve real world problems.

Conduct research to advance the state of the art in computing and intergrate research results and innovations into other scientific disciplines.

Provide computer education and training to students in other departments.

Provide students expertise to the people of Western and the nation.

The Vision of the School of Computing:


Build a strong research and teaching environment
