    Brief history

    Masinde Muliro University Of Science and Technology(MMUST) was founded on 5th January 1972 as Western College of Arts and Applied Sciences(WEKO). It was to provide a training ground for technical manpower requirements for the province and nation.In December 2002,WEKO became Western University College of Science and Technology(WUCST) as a constituent of Moi University. It became a fully acredited public university in 2007.



    School Location

    Masinde Muliro University of Science and Technology main campus is located in the western region specifically Kakamega county.The School of Computing is located inside the main campus in the university.The university is located on Kakamega-Webuye Main Road just about 10 kilometers from the Kakamega town center.The School of Computing is located in the Science Department Buliding (SPD) at the ground floor,where the chairman is also located.



    Launching

    The school of computing was launched last year from being a department.The department had more than 20 full time lecturers and about 10 part time lecturers.When it was made a school,the population of lecturers doubled and the services offered to the students were above standards.

    School Certification

    The school is certified by the education sector to teach the above programmes. The school offers E-Learning to the students far from the main campus. The school has also automated to teaching online.The students have student portals which they use to register for the courses they are undertaking.They access the timetables,their results and fee balances also through the portals.
